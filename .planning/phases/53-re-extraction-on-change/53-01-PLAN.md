<objective>
Harden re-extraction so a user-owned (user_modified) breakdown element's curated scene links are never wiped and recreated when the breakdown is re-extracted, and prove the full v6.0→v7.0 re-extraction chain (stale-on-change → re-extract → user edits preserved → stale cleared) with explicit tests.

Purpose: REEX-02 ("re-extraction refreshes the breakdown against the changed scene text without discarding user-added/edited breakdown elements") currently preserves a user_modified element's *description* (SYNC-01) but still reconciles its *scene links* on every re-extract — wiping and recreating the AI-sourced links underneath a user who has taken ownership of that element. This plan extends "preserve user edits" to the links that belong to those elements (D-53-01), and verifies REEX-01 (stale-on-change, already wired) end-to-end (D-53-02). No new behavior beyond this one additive guard (D-53-03).

Output: A surgical guard in the extract loop plus three new tests; existing breakdown tests stay green.
</objective>
